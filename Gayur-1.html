History

Subsidiary Limited Liability Company “Gayur-1” was founded in 2003 under the name of "Akmal" CJSC, in 2006 it was renamed Gayur LLC, in 2008 it was renamed "Amethyst Plus" CJSC, in 2009 it was renamed Gayur LLC ”, since April 2011 was renamed into DOOO “Gayur-1”.

DOOO "Gayur-1" is working on the direction of the production of building materials and the performance of general construction works:

  • Construction and reconstruction of buildings and structures;
  • Implementation of bank protection works;
  • Construction, reconstruction and maintenance of highways;
  • Performance of drilling and blasting works;
  • Production of inert materials (sand, crushed stone, chive and cement blocks);
  • Portland cement M-400 M-500;
  • Implementation of the functions of the Customer-developer.

Form of ownership: Private enterprise.

During the work in DOOO "Gayur 1" a team of highly qualified engineering and technical workers, working personnel has developed, which allows the company to perform work on its own with high quality and in a short time.

The company has modern equipment and tools necessary for the performance of general construction works.

During the production activity, the group of companies DOOO "Gayur 1" has established partnerships with a number of organizations that perform specialized work, supply equipment, materials and components, which allows you to perform the full range of construction and installation works with the delivery of the facility.

Competitors.

The construction system market began to take shape quite a long time ago. Since then, some of the first companies have proven their high professional status in practice, some enterprises, unable to withstand the competition, have collapsed. Although if the company is on the market not so long ago, it does not mean bad. New businesses are less conservative, open to new technologies and new ideas.

As it became clear, the level of competition in this field of activity is very high, and therefore it is very difficult for a young company to stay afloat when large organizations that have already proven themselves for 10-15 years offer their services in this industry. In such conditions, it is necessary to debug corporate work in a team, work professionally with clients, make competent advertising, and create an active policy of entering the market.In business, as in love and war, all means are good, as long as it does not go against the law, of course. For DOOO "Gayur-1", the main competitor in the field of construction is those who have good experience. Which has been in the construction market for a long time, and has established itself as an enterprise with excellent quality and excellent reputation.
